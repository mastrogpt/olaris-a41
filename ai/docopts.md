# Plugin ops ai

AI tasks to generate code.

## Synopsis

```text
Usage:
  ai chat <action> [<args>...]
  ai stream <action> [<args>...]
  ai new <action> [<package>]
  ai user (add|delete|update) <user>
  ai loader [--action=<action>] [--chunksize=<size>] [--collection=<name>] [--clean] [--opts=<opts>] <file>... 
```
